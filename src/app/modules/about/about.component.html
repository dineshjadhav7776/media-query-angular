Happy Clients

It started with a simple but profound observation in our own wedding planning journey. We saw and witnessed the exhaustion and frustration that couples go through in hopes of creating their dream wedding, all while maintaining a budget. “How many more people do I need to reach out to before we can book this service and check it off our list?” Going through this same stressful experience, we thought, “there must be a simpler way to do this!” .This was the start of lena, and we created this platform with the simple goal of making photographyplanning easier (and more fun!).
